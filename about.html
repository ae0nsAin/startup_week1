Our History

Los Pollos Hermanos was founded in Albuquerque, New Mexico, by Gustavo Fring and his trusted partners. Known for its delicious fried chicken and friendly service, the restaurant quickly became a community favorite.

Our Values

We believe in family, community, and quality. Each meal is prepared with love and care, bringing people together around the table.
